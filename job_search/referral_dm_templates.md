# 5 Referral DM Templates

**Universal rules**
- Send between **Tue–Thu, 9–11am or 4–6pm IST**. Higher reply rate.
- **Personalize the first line** with something specific from their profile/work. If you can't, don't send the DM.
- **Never ask for a job in the first message.** Ask for advice, a 15-min chat, or feedback.
- **Lead with one project link**, not your resume. Pick the one most relevant to *their* work.
- **Keep it under 120 words.** Long DMs get ignored.
- **Follow up once after 7 days**, never twice.

## Tracking your DMs

Maintain a sheet with: Date sent, Person, Company, Template used, Personalization line, Replied (Y/N), Outcome. After 50 DMs, look at reply rate by template and personalization quality. The data will tell you what's working — usually it's the personalization, not the template.
