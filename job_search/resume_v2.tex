\projectHeading{NyayaGPT --- Indian Legal LLM with QLoRA Fine-Tuning, Quantization Benchmark \& MLOps}{\href{https://github.com/gauravgarwal9011/NyayGPT}{GitHub} \,|\, \href{https://huggingface.co/gauravgarwal/NyayaGPT-Mistral7B-adapter}{HF Adapter}}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Fine-tuned \textbf{Mistral-7B-Instruct-v0.3} (Unsloth + QLoRA, r=16, $\alpha$=32, 2 epochs) into a specialized Indian legal assistant on 1{,}690 instruction pairs (1{,}521 train / 169 eval) scraped from IndianKanoon and augmented via Azure OpenAI GPT-4o; achieved \textbf{ROUGE-1 = 0.57}, \textbf{ROUGE-L = 0.40}, \textbf{RAGAS Faithfulness = 0.66} on the held-out set.

    \item Shipped end-to-end MLOps: MLflow tracking across training/evaluation/A-B experiments, Streamlit dashboard for live \textbf{vanilla Mistral Q4 vs NyayaGPT Q4} apples-to-apples comparison, reproducible training/quantization scripts, and public \textbf{Hugging Face Hub} release of the LoRA adapter with model card.
